Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This is a starting template. Have it reviewed by a qualified professional before relying on it, and replace the contact email and any bracketed placeholders.

Dupe It ("we", "us") helps you find products and cheaper lookalikes from a photo. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Photos you upload. The image you submit is sent to our AI provider to identify the item. We do not store your photos after the request is processed — they are used only to generate your results in that moment.
  • Search terms. We store a count of how often a given item name is searched (e.g. "silk slip dress") to power the "Most Popular" feature. This is not linked to you and contains no personal data or images.
  • Basic technical data. Your IP address and request metadata are processed transiently to apply rate limits and prevent abuse. We do not build advertising profiles.

Third parties we use

To provide the service we send the necessary data to:

  • Anthropic (Claude) — analyzes your photo to identify the item.
  • SerpAPI — retrieves shopping results for your search terms.
  • Vercel — hosts the application.
  • Upstash — stores anonymous search counts and rate-limit counters.

Each processes data under its own privacy terms. We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies & tracking

Dupe It does not set advertising cookies. If we add analytics in the future, we will update this policy.

Data retention

Uploaded photos are not retained. Anonymous search counts and rate-limit counters are kept only as long as needed for the feature (rate-limit counters expire within 48 hours).

Children

Dupe It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Your choices

Because we don't store your photos or maintain personal accounts (at this time), there is no personal profile to access or delete. For questions, contact us at [your-email@example.com].

Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date will change accordingly.
